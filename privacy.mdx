At [Paywalls.ai](https://paywalls.ai) ("we", "our"), we value the privacy and security of our users' and customers' personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your information when you access or use our services and platform.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect different types of data to provide our services efficiently and securely:

- **Personal Data:** Information you provide directly, such as payment preferences, contact details, and credentials to access the platform.
- **Usage Data:** Technical and interaction data with our platform, including access logs, tokens consumed, and usage metrics.
- **Automatically Collected Data:** We use cookies and similar technologies to collect data about browsing, performance, and use of our website to improve your experience and internal analytics.

## 2. Use of Information

The information we collect is used to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
- Manage billing and payments related to API and model usage.
- Personalize and optimize the user experience.
- Comply with legal and security obligations.
- Communicate with you about updates, support, and relevant offers.

## 3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted third-party providers and partners who help us operate, maintain, or improve our services, always under strict confidentiality and security agreements.

We may also share information **only when required by a competent authority with valid jurisdiction to request such data, or to protect our legal rights.** The processing of personal data is carried out under the legal responsibility of Grindery Inc., the operator of [Paywalls.ai](https://paywalls.ai).

## 4. Data Security

[Paywalls.ai](https://paywalls.ai) implements appropriate technical and organizational measures, including data encryption, to protect personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

## 5. User Rights

As a user, you have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal data. You can:

- Manage your data directly from the [Paywalls.ai](https://paywalls.ai) dashboard.
- Request total or partial deletion of your information and account closure by contacting our support.
- Receive confirmation of actions taken regarding your data.

## 6. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies to enhance the functionality and performance of our platform, as well as for analytics and personalization. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

## 7. Compliance

[Paywalls.ai](https://paywalls.ai), operated by Grindery Inc., complies with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), ensuring your rights are respected under these legal frameworks.
